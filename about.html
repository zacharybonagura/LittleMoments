About Us

Little Moments is an alternative rock band forged in the heart of West Nyack, NY. With a sound shaped by grit, melody, and emotion, the band channels life’s chaos into raw, resonant songs that cut deep. Every lyric, every riff is a reflection of late nights, unspoken thoughts, and the moments that slip through the cracks. Fueled by a relentless passion for music and an instinct to be heard, Little Moments doesn’t just play - they feel, they bleed, and they leave it all on stage.
